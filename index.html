Defence Industries Corporation of Nigeria Cooperative Sales Dashboard

This project focuses on developing an interactive Excel dashboard to track and analyze cooperative sales performance for the Defence Industries Corporation of Nigeria. The dashboard was designed to provide stakeholders with clear insights into transactions, revenue, profit trends, and demographic sales analysis, enabling data-driven decision-making.
Key Features Total Sales Metrics – Displays total transactions, quantities, revenue, and profit/loss. Profit Trend Analysis – Visualizes monthly profit trends across 2015–2016. Demographic Insights – Breakdowns by age group and gender to understand consumer patterns. Product-Level Analysis – Compares revenue and profit across major product categories (Accessories, Clothing, Phones). Regional Sales Performance – Highlights top 10 performing states by sales volume. Dynamic Filters (Slicers) – Enables interactive filtering by: Sales Person Payment Option Age Group
Business Impact The dashboard provided the cooperative management with a comprehensive view of performance across multiple dimensions, helping to: Identify profitable product categories. Track sales contribution across states and salespersons. Understand demographic buying behavior for better targeting. Make evidence-based decisions that support growth..
